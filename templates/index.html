About Herstory Walks

Herstory Walks is a platform designed to connect women travelers with historical sites that celebrate the lives and legacies of extraordinary women throughout history. Our mission is to make history more inclusive and inspiring by curating destinations that highlight women's contributions to culture, science, politics, and beyond. Whether you're exploring iconic landmarks or uncovering hidden gems, Herstory Walks offers an engaging way to experience the past through the lens of women's achievements. Embark on a journey where every step tells a story of resilience, innovation, and empowerment.
