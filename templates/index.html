About Herstory Walks

Herstory Walks is a platform designed to connect women travelers with historical sites that celebrate the lives and legacies of extraordinary women throughout history. Our mission is to make history more inclusive and inspiring by curating destinations that highlight women's contributions to culture, science, politics, and beyond. Whether you're exploring iconic landmarks or uncovering hidden gems, Herstory Walks offers an engaging way to experience the past through the lens of women's achievements.

Embark on a journey where every step tells a story of resilience, innovation, and empowerment. Our platform provides carefully researched guides, personalized itineraries, and insightful historical context to help travelers deepen their understanding of the impact women have had across generations and cultures.

We believe that by shining a light on these incredible stories, we can inspire future generations to walk confidently in the footsteps of those who paved the way. Whether you're a solo traveler, part of a group, or simply passionate about women's history, Herstory Walks offers an enriching and meaningful travel experience that connects the past with the present.

Join us in discovering the untold stories that deserve to be remembered and celebrated.
